Reversible crochet octopus Materials Steps Video

Making the body

  • make a magic ring
  • single crochet 8 in magic ring
  • increase 8 times
  • alternate sc and inc 8 times
  • single crochet twice than inc 8 times
  • sc three times than inc 8 times
  • sc four times than inc 8 times
  • single crochet for 5 rows
  • sc four times and than decrease 8 times

Making the tentacles

  • single crochet twice
  • chain 7
  • sc 4 rows
  • repeat 8 times

Assembling the octopus

  • stitch the faces on the body
  • take yarn of either colours on a needle and stitch together the two pieces
  • when almost completely stitched, add poly fill
  • stitch it fully aaaand....

YOU`RE DONE!
